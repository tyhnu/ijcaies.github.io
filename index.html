About

Welcome to 2025 International Conference on Future AI - Ethics and Safety

Sponsored by the National Nature Science Foundation of China, this conference focuses on the cutting-edge integration of robotics and large-scale artificial intelligence models (such as generative AI and embodied intelligence). It will cover key technologies and applications including intelligent control, multimodal perception, human-robot collaboration, autonomous systems, and AI-powered robotic platforms.

The conference aims to bring together leading researchers, engineers, and practitioners from academia and industry to discuss the latest advances, exchange innovative ideas, and explore potential collaborations in the rapidly evolving field of AI and robotics.

With the theme of "Building Safe and Ethical AI for Human-Machine Coexistence", the conference will feature keynote speeches by renowned experts, technical paper presentations, panel discussions, and exhibition opportunities.

Conference Topic

  • Artificial Intelligence and Societal Needs
  • Biomimetic and Humanoid Robotics
  • Embodied Intelligence and Robotic Application
