    2025 International Conference on AI
    - Ethics and Safety - 
    Oct. 25-26, 2025 | Hong Kong

    Welcome to 2025 International Conference on AI - Ethics and Safety 

    Sponsored by the National Nature Science Foundation of China, this conference focuses on the cutting-edge integration of robotics and large-scale artificial intelligence models (such as generative AI and embodied intelligence). It will cover key technologies and applications including intelligent control, multimodal perception, human-robot collaboration, autonomous systems, and AI-powered robotic platforms. Special attention will be given to issues of safety, interpretability, and ethics in deploying large AI models within robotic systems, with the goal of ensuring trustworthy, safe, and responsible technological development in a rapidly evolving landscape.

    The conference will bring together leading experts and scholars from around the world to establish a high-level platform for academic exchange, showcase the latest research advancements, and promote interdisciplinary collaboration. Ultimately, it aims to lay a solid foundation for advancing robotics and artificial intelligence and for building sustainable and trustworthy intelligent systems.
